About Me

My name is Stephanie Babb, and I am a first year student in the ATLAS Creative Technology and Design master's program. I completed my undergraduate degree in computer science with focuses in computer systems and human-computer interaction. I grew up in Washington, completed my undergrad in Oregon, and spent the past 3 years working in Idaho. I like to say I have collected all of the pacific northwest states. I have a passion for sustainability and accessibility and hope to incorporate principles of both into my future work. In my free time I enjoy watching football, baseball, and women's gymnastics; doing nail art; and getting outside on a good walk when I can.
